denizen

Denizen is a pre-alpha work in progress. It lacks most of the features advertised here. You can see the progress on my to-do list.

Denizen is yet another blog CMS a personal website engine that connects you to the IndieWeb with no configuration, no connection strings, and hopefully for free. Find it on Codeberg.

> Get started

Denizen is written in JavaScript for Deno, and can be deployed on Deno Deploy or on any server — lowering the barrier of having a personal website without the limitations of static blog generators.

Supported protocols and what they do

  • Webmention: liking and commenting across websites
  • Webactions: interact with other websites with a single click
  • Micropub: post from a variety of web, mobile, desktop and command-line apps
  • IndieAuth: log in to apps with your own website
  • Microsub: follow other people's websites not yet!
